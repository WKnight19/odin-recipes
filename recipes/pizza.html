Pizza!

Description

Pizza is a deliscious treat that everyone loves! From the crust to the toppings, pizza is a versatile dish that can be customized to your liking. Originating fron Italy, pizza has become a worldwide favorite. Now, pizza rules everywhere from the finest resturaunts to the streets of New York and Chicago!

Ingredients

  • one roll of pizza dough
  • a half cup of tomato sauce
  • a whole cup of whole milk mozzarella cheese
  • a quarter cup of your favorite toppings
  • basil and olive oil...a must for any true Pizza!

Steps:

  1. kneed the pizza dough into your desired thickness
  2. preheat the oven to 500 degrees
  3. layer the bottom of the pizza with olive oil, then apply the tomato sauce
  4. Spread out all of the cheeese on top of the pizza
  5. Coat the pizza with the toppings, and slide it in the oven
  6. keep an eye on the oven, until the crust is golden brown and the cheese starts to bubble and brown
  7. place pizza on the drying rack, and coat with the basil and olive oil (HINT: More is better)
  8. slice the pizza into even slices, let cool for two minutes, and enjoy!
