Chicken and Rice!

Description

Chicken and rice has long been the go-to meal for people dedicated to fitness and health. low in calories, high in protein, and more than customizable, chicken and rice is a staple everywhere from the gym to the dinner table. In fact, it is so popular in part for being so easy to cook. Dont belive me? You wont believe how well you made it taste after the first try! Let's try it out and get those gains!!!

Ingredients

  • a half pound of raw chicken breast
  • one-third a cup of white or brown rice
  • two-thirds a cup of water
  • salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder
  • olive oil and some Frank's Red Hot...a must for any true Chicken and Rice dish!

Steps:

  1. boil the water in a saucepan over the stove
  2. preheat a non-stick skillet over medium high heat for a few minutes, heating up a small amount of butter
  3. season the chicken breast with all aformentioned spices, and then rub it in a few teaspoons of olive oil
  4. when the water is boiling, add the rice, some salt to taste, cover the top, and let rest for about 15-20 minutes
  5. put the chicken on the skillet when smoking, making sure to put the flattest side down first. leave still for six minutes
  6. after the six minutes, flip the chicken, cover it with a lid, and let cook for another five minutes
  7. after the chicken is done, lay on a dry paper towel for a few minutes to cool (HINT: Do Not Cut)
  8. Remove the rice, and roll it around in the skillet to soak up the chicken juices, Plate and enjoy!
